Our Story

Three Generations of Power

Central Valley Power Electric is a family business that spans three generations and four decades of electrical work. What started in Monterey in 1979 now serves Fresno's homes, farms, factories, and businesses.

1979
The First Generation
The family's electrical business is founded in Monterey, California — building a reputation for quality work on the Central Coast.
2000s
Second Generation Grows
The business expands across California. The next generation joins, bringing new skills to residential, commercial, and industrial markets.
2020
CV Power Electric Founded
CEO Michael — 3rd generation, 25+ years experience — establishes Central Valley Power Electric in Fresno to serve the entire Central Valley.
Today
Serving All of Fresno
Top 10% CA licensed contractor. Residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural clients across the Central Valley.
